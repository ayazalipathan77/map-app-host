About SMART KMC

SMART KMC is a cutting-edge project designed to revolutionize urban management and public services within the Karachi Metropolitan Corporation. By leveraging advanced mapping technologies, we provide comprehensive, real-time data to enhance decision-making, improve infrastructure, and foster a more connected and efficient city for all its residents. Our platform aims to streamline operations, increase transparency, and empower citizens with accessible information.

Our Services

Geospatial Data Management

Centralized system for collecting, storing, and managing vast amounts of geospatial data related to urban infrastructure, demographics, and environmental factors.

Real-time Urban Monitoring

Live tracking and visualization of key urban metrics, including traffic flow, public transport, and utility services, enabling proactive management.

Public Service Optimization

Tools to optimize routes for waste collection, emergency services, and maintenance crews, leading to faster response times and reduced operational costs.

Citizen Engagement Platform

An intuitive interface for citizens to report issues, access public information, and provide feedback, fostering community participation.

Infrastructure Planning & Development

Advanced analytics and visualization tools to support urban planners in making informed decisions for sustainable development and infrastructure projects.

Environmental Monitoring

Monitoring of environmental parameters like air quality, water resources, and green spaces to support ecological conservation efforts.
